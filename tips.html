TIPS

Hello everyone, here are some tips that will help you ease up and improve your workout pattern.

  • Before starting any workout routine, make sure to do some streatching from toe to head.
  • Best way to build muscle, increase strength and endure more stress is to do the exercises slowly
  • Try to be consistant and take as less as possible resting time in between exercises.
  • Before progressing to higher difficulty level, make sure that you can go atleast 2-3 sets of the exercises from the previous difficulty
  • Best way to go up to 2-3 sets is to slowly increase the Repeatations.
  • Try to drink less water in between workouts
